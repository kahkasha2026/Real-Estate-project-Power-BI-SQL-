📊 **Real Estate Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI Project**

While working on this dashboard, I found some interesting patterns that really show how data can drive smarter decisions 👇

**🔑 KPIs Tracked:**

* Total Sales: **2.08M**
* Target: **1.84M**
* Revenue: **2.68M**
* Total Properties: **275**
* Occupancy Status: **47 Occupied | 53 Vacant | 35 Unsold**

**🔍 Key Insights I Found:**

* Sales have already **exceeded the target**, which is a strong positive signal ✅
* A major contribution is coming from **top agents (Richard leading with ~928K)**
* I noticed a **sales dip around Sep–Nov**, followed by a sharp rise in **December** 📈
* A significant number of properties are still **vacant or unsold**, indicating scope for better utilization

**⚠️ Business Problem:**

* No clear visibility of **sales performance vs targets**
* Hard to track **agent contribution & monthly trends**
* Lack of insights into **property occupancy and inventory gaps**

**💡 Solution I Built:**

* Designed an interactive dashboard to monitor **KPIs, sales trends, and agent performance**
* Added **target vs actual comparison** for quick decision-making
* Visualized **property status** to highlight unsold inventory

**✅ Impact:**

* Helped identify **high-performing agents & weak sales months**
* Enabled better **sales planning and inventory management**
* Turned raw data into **actionable insights for business growth** 🚀
